To verify the installation, run:

  kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "kafka-partition-remapper-operator.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"

The operator is now watching for the following custom resources:
  - KafkaPartitionRemapper

To create a partition remapper proxy, apply a KafkaPartitionRemapper resource:

  apiVersion: kafka.oso.sh/v1alpha1
  kind: KafkaPartitionRemapper
  metadata:
    name: my-remapper
  spec:
    replicas: 2
    listen:
      port: 9092
    kafka:
      bootstrapServers:
        - "kafka:9092"
    mapping:
      virtualPartitions: 1000
      physicalPartitions: 100

To check the status of your remapper:

  kubectl get kafkapartitionremappers
  kubectl describe kafkapartitionremapper my-remapper

For more information, visit:
  https://github.com/osodevops/kafka-partition-remapper-operator
